## About

The Centre for Open Societal Systems (COSS) drives global societal transformation by facilitating countries to develop their Digital Public Infrastructure and crafting compelling change narratives for adoption of Digital Public Goods to create impact at scale.

## Mission

<!-- Mission Image Placeholder -->
<div align="center">
  <img src="./assets/images/mission-image.png" alt="Mission" width="600">
</div>

Our mission is to enable equitable, inclusive societal transformation by providing trusted technical guidance & co-creating digital solutions to advance the global adoption of Digital Public Goods (DPGs). We aim to improve the lives of 1 billion people globally by 2030 through adoption of Digital Public Goods (DPGs).
